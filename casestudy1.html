The Problem

My friend Sandra unintentionally presented me with a problem I could solve for her while our kids were playing one day. She is a photographer and wants to really launch her business but she doesn't quite know how to do it. She has many different social media outlets for her business, but she doesn't update it consistently, plus she'd like just one central place to steer people to when they want a professional photographer. She also has many different colors and fonts and logos in many different places such as on social media and business cards - nothing is consistent.

I presented the idea of having one central website, one central logo, one set of colors and fonts, and then the off-shoots of different social media platforms would unilaterally be updated and connected from there and vice-versa. She loved this idea, so I went to work!
